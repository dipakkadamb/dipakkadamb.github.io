Why Local AI Instead of Cloud AI?

Cloud AI tools like ChatGPT are powerful — but they come with serious corporate risks that a local LLM completely eliminates.

Cloud AI: Your Data Goes Abroad

Every prompt sent to ChatGPT or Claude is processed on overseas servers. Confidential contracts, customer data, internal strategies — all potentially stored by a foreign company.

Local AI: Complete Data Sovereignty

Every query stays on your server. No vendor sees your data. Fully compliant with GDPR, HIPAA, and India's IT Act 2000. Perfect for regulated industries.

Cloud AI: Expensive Subscriptions

₹2,000–₹8,000 per user per month. For a 20-person team that's ₹40,000–₹1,60,000 every single month — growing as your team grows. Enterprise plans cost lakhs annually.

Local AI: One-Time Setup Cost

Pay once for installation. Unlimited queries forever — ₹0 per token. ROI is typically achieved within 4–6 months. Your entire team uses it with no per-seat fees.

Cloud AI: Internet Dependency

No internet = no AI. Vendor outages, API rate limits, and downtime affect your entire team's productivity. You control nothing about availability.

Local AI: Always On, Always Yours

Works on your internal network 24/7 — even during internet outages. No vendor SLA to depend on. Your server, your uptime, your control.

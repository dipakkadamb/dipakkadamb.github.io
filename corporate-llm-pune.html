What Is a Corporate Local LLM?

A Corporate Local LLM is a powerful AI language model — like ChatGPT, but installed entirely on your own server inside your office or data centre. No data ever leaves your premises. No subscriptions. No vendor lock-in. Your employees get a private, intelligent AI assistant that knows your business — because it only runs on your hardware.

What We Set Up For You

Hardware Assessment & Sizing

We audit your existing servers or recommend new hardware (GPU workstations, NVMe SSDs, RAM upgrades) optimised for your chosen LLM model size and team usage.

Model Installation & Optimisation

Full Ollama / LM Studio setup with your chosen model (LLaMA 3, Mistral, Phi-3, Gemma, DeepSeek, Qwen). We apply quantisation (GGUF Q4/Q8) for the best speed-accuracy balance on your hardware.

Network Isolation & Security

Firewall rules, VLAN isolation, and access-control policies ensure only authorised users on your internal network can query the AI. Zero external exposure.

Private Chat UI (Open WebUI)

Deploy a beautiful, private ChatGPT-like interface on your intranet. Multi-user support, conversation history, file uploads — all running on your own server, accessible from any device on your network.

RAG — AI That Knows Your Business

Connect the LLM to your internal documents, SOPs, PDFs, manuals, and knowledge base using Retrieval-Augmented Generation. Ask questions about your own company data — privately.

Staff Training & Full Handover

Comprehensive onboarding for your IT team plus an all-staff walkthrough. Full documentation — architecture diagrams, update procedures, troubleshooting guides — handed to you on Day 7.

Why Local Instead of Cloud AI?

Cloud AI tools like ChatGPT are powerful — but they come with real corporate risks that on-premise LLMs completely eliminate.

Cloud AI: Data Privacy Risk

Every prompt sent to ChatGPT/Claude can potentially be used for model training or exposed in breaches. Your confidential business data is at risk.

Local LLM: Complete Data Sovereignty

Every query stays on your server. No vendor sees your data. Fully compliant with GDPR, HIPAA, and IT Act 2000.

Cloud AI: Recurring Subscription Costs

₹2,000–₹8,000 per user/month adds up fast. Enterprise plans cost lakhs annually and scale with usage.

Local LLM: One-Time Setup Cost

Pay once for installation. Run unlimited queries forever with zero per-token charges. ROI typically achieved within 4–6 months.

Cloud AI: Internet Dependency

No internet = no AI. Downtime at the vendor affects your entire team's productivity. You control nothing.

Local LLM: Always-On, Offline Access

Works on your internal network 24/7. No internet required. No downtime because of vendor outages.

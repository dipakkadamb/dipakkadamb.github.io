Zoho CRM Automation Suite

End-to-end Zoho CRM customization with multi-app workflows, automated lead capture, and Deluge-powered business logic handling vast datasets seamlessly.

The Problem

Our client's sales representatives were losing over 20 hours a week to manual data entry. Leads coming from Facebook Ads, web forms, and direct emails had to be manually inputted, categorized, and assigned. This created huge delays in response times and severely damaged conversion rates.

The Solution

I engineered a fully comprehensive Zoho ecosystem overhaul. By utilizing Webhooks and Zoho Flow, lead capture was automated instantaneously.

Next, I embedded complex Deluge Scripting logic to auto-route leads to the appropriate salesperson based on territory codes, while simultaneously dispatching automated introductory emails tailored to the lead's exact product inquiry. Furthermore, the pipeline automatically syncs billed deals to Zoho Books.

Measurable Outcomes

  • - Decreased initial lead response time from 4 hours to roughly 3 seconds.
  • - Eliminated 100% of pipeline manual data entry errors.
  • - Recovered approximately ~$2k worth of payroll hours previously lost to admin tasks monthly.
